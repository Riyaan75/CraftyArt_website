These are few of our best handicraft Products from across India

Himanchal Pradesh

The various kinds of handicrafts available in Himachal Pradesh include woodwork, leather embroidery, metal wares, carpets, painting and woolen textiles. The crafts of this state are vast and portray the artistic skill of the craftsmen. Mention must be bade of Thangka which is an intricate and brightly colored painting done on cloth by Tibetan artisans.

Rajasthan

Rajasthan is the largest producer of handicraft items in India. The art and craft of Rajasthan is known around the world. Tourists visiting India always desire to take back souvenirs of Rajasthani handicrafts. Many bright colored and patterned crafts of the state are used to adorn household as interior decoration.

Jammu and Kashmir

The handicrafts of Jammu & Kashmir include some vibrant hand-made crafts, which symbolize the cultural significance of the state. The craftsmen of Jammu and Kashmir have mastered in making items like carpets, baskets, wall hangings, shawls and much more. The handicraft industry of the state is thriving and it provides employment to a large mass of local people of the valley.

Punjab

The rich tradition of Punjab handicrafts includes Phulkari along with Pidhis, Jootis, Durries and Parandis. Phulkari is however the most popular of them all. Many of the crafts are used as household items or decorative items in houses and portray the colorful identity of the state.

Uttar Pradesh

Uttar Pradesh produces large number of crafts created by the large number of artisans spread across the state. Various handicrafts including earthen pottery, carpets weaving, chikankari embroidery, silk saris and more, are the primary artifacts. Different parts of the state produce different crafts. Varanasi is popular for silk saris, Mirzapur produces carpets, Agra and Kanpur are famous for its leather crafts, Moradabad manufactures metal ware and Lucknow produces chikankari embroidery, which is renown all over the world.
